Year 2
What are your SMART objectives for Year 2?
SMART Objective #1
Secure a remote position in my desired field before January 2025 by applying for a minimum of 6 openings per week, facilitated by Coventry's Talent Team.
SMART Objective #2
To enhance my interview performance, I will dedicate 2 hours per week to company research and conduct 2 mock interviews with industry experts before every interview. This approach aligns with career success and readiness for interviews by highlighting strengths beforehand (Hardavella et al., 2016).
What do you need to do to prepare yourself and your environment to achieve your objectives for Year 2?
Solid references are key to successful job applications (Schooley, 2023). I will reach out and secure highly valued references within my fields to boost my applications.
What new skills do you want to learn?
I would like to learn effective writing skills to produce better quality CVs and cover letters. I will do this by meeting with Coventry's talent team for writing advice.
What new skills, knowledge do you need to possess?
Patience is vital within the job application process (Indeed, 2023). This process is often very competitive and lengthy, however, maintaining a positive outlook and making informed decisions will prove valuable in securing the right job.
What strategies will you utilise and why, to get from where you are now to achieve your Year 1 aims?
To optimise job applications, I'll craft customised cover letters for each role, addressing specific requirements of employers. Additionally, I'll employ my network and work with recruitment agencies to broaden my job prospects and enhance my chances of success.
What existing skills do you need to develop? What resources will use to help you attain your goals for Year 1?
Following network mapping reflection, I aim to develop my networking to gain more opportunities for feedback and career advice.
