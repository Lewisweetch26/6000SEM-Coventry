SMART Objective #1
Specific : Select portfolio pieces to present to recruiters

Measurable : Before graduation (July) curate a collection of 5 portfolio pieces to show off my individual skills and knowledge.

Achievable : Evaluating finished pieces that I will have completed since beginning to code and identifying the top five most impressive.

Relevant : Selecting the right pieces which include large portions of my own creativity and knowledge will reflect my capabilities to an employer.

Time-bound : I will have my pieces selected by graduation.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : Create a portfolio website.

Measurable : 1 month from graduation complete a working portfolio website which easily showcases the pieces selected.

Achievable : I will use my knowledge and experience in building website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create this website.

Relevant : A website dedicated with all my projects in one place will make it seamless for employers. (reference portfolios being good for applications).

Time-bound : I will have a live portfolio website by August 2024.
SMART Objective #3
Specific : Reach out to industry links for feedback to refine portfolio.

Measurable : After completing my website I will forward it to 4 network links over both industries who I trust to gather feedback for improvement.

Achievable : My network maps shows many trusted experienced individuals in the industries.

Relevant : With their feedback I can refine the website to my work is presented in the best possible way. ( reference feedback being good).

Time-bound : Gathering feedback and making improvements will take 1 month and be completed by September.
