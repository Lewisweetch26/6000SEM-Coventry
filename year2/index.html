Year 2
Obtain a remote job in Coding or Marketing firm based in America
Preparation
Routine Set-up: Prioritising job search and interview preparation with a stable schedule encouraging focus and productivity.
Prep References: Identify references who can vouch for my skills and work attitude.
Strategies
Tailored Applications: Create unique cover letters for each role to meet the specific needs of the employers.
Network-Based job search: Leverage my network and recruitment agencies to expand job opportunities and increase chance of success.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : I will actively search and apply for remote positions in my coding or marketing. Remote positions allow me to join an American company prior to relocation, setting foundations while offering freedom to travel year-round.

Measurable : I will apply for 6 openings each week in each field

Achievable : I will enhance my job search by reaching out to Coventry's talent team to update my CV and online presence.

Relevant : Obtaining a job in one of these industries provides me with experience in-line with my career aspirations and offers opportunities to grow and understand the industries more.

Time-bound : I target to receive a job offer before the end of 2024 to start work in 2025.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : To increase the likelihood of receiving a job offer I will thoroughly prepare for interviews using mock interview scenarios and sessions dedicated to researching companies.

Measurable : I will hold 2 mock interviews with industry experts from my network map before every interview I receive.

Achievable : I will seek guidance from those who have gone through similar interviews and spend 2 hours weekly answering common interview questions related to these industries.

Relevant : Preparing for interview reference.

Time-bound : I will conduct 2 mock interviews within a week before of an interview date.
Resources
Network Expansion: Leveraging existing relationships to grow my professional network for future job opportunities and industry insights.

Application Tracking: Utilise Microsoft applications and folders to organise job applications and contact details for future reference.
