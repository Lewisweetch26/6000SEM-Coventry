Year 2
Obtain a remote job in Coding or Marketing firm based in America
Preparation
Routine Set-up: Prioritising job search and interview preparation with a stable schedule encouraging focus and productivity.
Prep References: Identify references who can vouch for my skills and work attitude.
Strategies
Tailored Applications: Create unique cover letters for each role to meet the specific needs of the employers.
Network-Based job search: Leverage my network and recruitment agencies to expand job opportunities and increase chance of success.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : I will actively search and apply for remote positions in desired fields. Remote positions allow me to join an American company prior to relocation, while offering freedom to travel year-round.

Measurable : I will apply for 6 openings each week in each field

Achievable : I can use Coventry's talent team to update my CV and online presence.

Relevant : Obtaining a job in these industries provides experience in-line with my career aspirations.

Time-bound : I aim to receive a job offer before January 2025.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : I will prepare for interviews using mock interviews and 2 hours of weekly prep dedicated to researching companies to increase chances of success.

Measurable : I will hold 2 mock interviews with my network industry experts before every interview.

Achievable : I will seek guidance from industry links and find common interview questions related to these industries online.

Relevant : Identifying and leveraging your strengths prior to interviews is key to any career path (Hardavella et al., 2016).

Time-bound : I will conduct 2 mock interviews within a week before each interview.
Resources
Network Expansion: Leveraging existing relationships to grow my professional network for future job opportunities and industry insights.

Application Tracking: Utilise Microsoft applications and folders to organise job applications and contact details for future reference.
