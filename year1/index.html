Preparation
Fast-Tracking Completion: I'll seek early feedback from my network connections to avoid delays while using folders for efficient access to projects, minimising search time.
Strategies
Scheduling: I'll apply Chidambaram's insights (2023) by introducing designated working hours into my daily routine for improved focus and task completion.
Phone Removal: (Webb, 2017) discusses a study where leaving your phone in another room can increase brain function and productivity.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : Select 4 previously completed projects which demonstrate skills required within my desired fields.

Measurable : Short-list 8 projects to make a final decision on 4.

Achievable : I've gained experience and understanding in both fields and will save time by only choosing completed projects.

Relevant : Employers can see the skills gained by using recently completed projects.

Time-bound : I will select these projects by August 2024.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : Receive 8 pieces of industry expert feedback to refine my completed website.

Measurable : Reach out to 8-12 coding/marketing industry connections to gather feedback.

Achievable : I have multiple connections to reach out to via my network map.

Relevant : Feedback can ensure my work is displayed professionally. The information received can be very effective (Wisniewski, 2020).

Time-bound : I will gather feedback and adjust my website by September 2024.
Resources
Industry Connections: I'll tap into my professional network for portfolio feedback.

Online Resources: I'll use platforms like LinkedIn and Handshake for portfolio guidance.

University Support: I'll attend meetings with the university talent team for advice on building CVs, cover letters and portfolios.
