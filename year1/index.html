Preparation
Fast-Tracking Completion: I'll seek early feedback from my network connections to avoid delays. I'll also organise work pieces in a folder for efficient access, minimising search time.
Strategies
Scheduling: I'll apply Chidambaram's insights (2023) by incorporating designated working hours into my daily routine for improved focus and task completion.
Phone Removal: (Webb, 2017) discusses a study where leaving your phone in another room can increase brain function and productivity.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : Select 4 completed projects created by myself in my final year of university which demonstrate skills required within my desired fields.

Measurable : Short-list 8 projects to make a final decision on 4.

Achievable : Ensure I understand the skills required in Coding and Marketing and are choosing projects from a completed list rather than creating new ones.

Relevant : Selecting projects from this specific timeframe will showcase progress to employers as I learn new coding languages and skills during this time.

Time-bound : These 4 projects will be selected within 2 months of graduation.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : Reach out and receive 8 pieces of feedback from industry experts to refine my completed website.

Measurable : Reach out to 8-12 coding/marketing industry connections to gather feedback.

Achievable : Utilising my network map, I will gain constructive criticism from trusted industry connections.

Relevant : Feedback can be used to refine the website portfolio to ensure my work is displayed professionally. Feedback is powerful and the information received can be very effective (Wisniewski, 2020).

Time-bound : I will gather feedback and make adjustment to have my website ready by September 2024 to apply and start work in 2025.
Resources
Industry Connections: I'll tap into my professional network for portfolio feedback.

Online Resources: I'll use platforms like LinkedIn and Handshake for portfolio guidance.

University Support: I'll attend meetings with the university talent team for advice on building CVs, cover letters and portfolios.
