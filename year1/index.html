Resources
This goal is solely around forming good habits. Using atomic habits as a resource to put good study habits in place to give myself the best chance to succeed. Even with high levels of motivation I struggle to stay focus on a particular activity that is causing discomfort (difficult assignments), developing my focus will help me make use of the time allocated more effectively.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : Achieve 70-80% in each module this during this year

Measurable : Using assignment briefs to make sure I am hitting everything needed to hit this grade.

Achievable : Attend all classes online, (reference why attending classes increases grade) seek help during catch ups sessions each fortnight, allow 4 hours each day to work on assignments and hand them in on time.

Relevant : Not hitting this grade this year will mean I will average lower than a 1st for my degree.

Time-bound : Accomplish this goal by the end of academic year, checking in regularly throughout the year with my success coach and professors.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : I will ensure I am getting enough sleep each night to improve my study abilities when awake.

Measurable : I will use a habit tracker to monitor how many hours of sleep I’m getting each night.

Achievable : I will put away electronic devices 30 minutes before bedtime, maintain a sleep schedule and be in bed by 10pm, ensure my room is dark and cool to improve sleep.

Relevant : Without proper sleep, the brain struggles to function at peak performance throughout the day when I’m working on assignments (reference why sleep improves work).

Time-bound : I will achieve this goal within the first semester recording 7 hours of sleep a night to help me throughout the rest of the academic year.
SMART Objective #3
Specific : I will allocate sufficient time to completing university assignments.

Measurable : Track the number of hours spent each day and compare to weekly target.

Achievable : Create a weekly schedule which prioritises university assignments as well as leaving room for other tasks I want/need to complete.

Relevant : Spending consistent time each week doing assignments is directly linked to finishing each assignment on time.

Time-bound : I will achieve this goal over the next semester using the weekly schedule and allowing time for activities which I enjoy ensuring good mental health.
