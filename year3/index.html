Year 3
Relocate to America
Preparation
Arrange Meetings: Meet with friends with approved visas for guidance and collect required documents for a faster application.
Strategies
Organisation: Applying lessons from year 1's scheduling strategy for productivity and SMART objective completion.
SMART Objective #1
Specific : Obtain the correct U.S. visa to ensure the relocation process is swift.

Measurable : Find exact number of documents required to complete visa application.

Achievable : Required documents can be obtained easily from immigration websites and current American remote job will provide proof of financials and reasoning.

Relevant : Focus on Business (2020) studies digital marketers salaries around the world. America offers nearly double the wage the UK offers.

Time-bound : Receive an approved visa by December 2025.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : Find three possible cities for relocation, considering future job opportunities, average salary and cost of living.

Measurable : Narrow down three states using the same factors.

Achievable : I will speak with American connections for advice, and I visit the cities individually.

Relevant : Retrieving location information and deciding ahead of time will allow the relocation process to be smooth and quick.

Time-bound : Finalise the list by June 2025, make all visits by December 2026.
Resources
Connect with Real Estate Agents: Contact multiple agents in advance to access new property listings.

Utilising Study Abroad Experience: Using my experience in the U.S. to quickly adapt to the new environment.
