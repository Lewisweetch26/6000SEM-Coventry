Year 3
What are your SMART objectives for Year 3?
SMART Objective #1
Secure the appropriate U.S. visa for smooth relocation before December 2025 by obtaining all necessary documents using online immigration sources. The U.S. offers higher salaries for digital marketer positions (Focus on Business, 2020) providing more financial stability.
SMART Objective #2
Using advice from American connections I would conduct comprehensive research and visit three prospective cities in the U.S. for relocation before December 2025, considering factors such as cost of living, future job opportunities, and average salaries.
What do you need to do to prepare yourself and your environment to achieve your objectives for Year 2?
Moving to a different country can be very costly (Leasca, 2023). I will save during my first two years of the plan to allow for settled living within the first few months of relocation.
What new skills do you want to learn?
Some visas require interviews with immigration officers. I will learn visa interview skills by reaching out to friends who have had similar interviews to understand and prepare myself.
What new skills, knowledge do you need to possess?
I will apply effective time management and organisation skills learned from my job research reflection. These are vital for meeting visa application deadlines and achieving my SMART goals promptly.
What strategies will you utilise and why, to get from where you are now to achieve your Year 1 aims?
Having several connections who have obtained visas can be useful. I will arrange meetings with them to understand the process that I will embark.
What existing skills do you need to develop? What resources will use to help you attain your goals for Year 1?
I will need to develop my research skills to ensure I find and complete all documents needed to obtain an approved visa. I have experience in documentation research from my year abroad however, this is more extensive.
