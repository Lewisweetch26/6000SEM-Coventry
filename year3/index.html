SMART Objective #1
Specific : Relocate to united states.

Measurable : Obtain all the required documents such as visas and or permits, secure accommodation.

Achievable : I have friends who have already gone through this process who can make this process easier. My girlfriend also is an American citizen which offers some help in my applications.

Relevant : Living in America increases my chances of securing a high-paying coding/marketing job due to the higher level of opportunities and higher salaries.

Time-bound : I will complete this move by the end of 2025.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : I will actively search for a coding/marketing role in America.

Measurable : I will apply for 6 openings each week in each field.

Achievable : I will use Coventry’s facilities to ensure my resume and LinkedIn profile are highlighting my skills in the best way. I will network with my connections in America to see who else I can meet through them.

Relevant : Securing this job role provides me with the experience to move on and merge both of my passions.

Time-bound : I will secure a job offer within one of these fields before 3 months of living in America.
SMART Objective #3
Specific : I will prepare properly for interviews to increase the chances of success.

Measurable : I will speak with fellow industry connections to hold mock interviews and gain feedback/tips from them to improve.

Achievable : I will dedicate set hours each week to practice common interview questions and prep myself on the companies I’m applying for.

Relevant : The first face-to-face interaction is the most important for securing a job (reference).

Time-bound : Every interview I receive I will conduct 1 mock interview 3 days before.
