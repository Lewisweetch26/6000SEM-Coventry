Year 3
Relocate to America
Preparation
Arrange Meetings: Meet with friends with approved visas to learn the process about the process and collect required documents for a faster application.
Strategies
Organisation: Applying lessons from year 1's scheduling strategy or productivity and SMART objective completion
SMART Objective #1
Specific : Obtain the correct U.S. visa to ensure the relocation process is swift.

Measurable : Provide proof of current job position obtained in the last year to demonstrate financial support and reasoning for staying.

Achievable : Complete required documents and information which can be obtained from immigration websites and connections based in America.

Relevant : America offers a lot opportunities within these fields with increased salaries (Reference).

Time-bound : Receive an approved visa by the end of 2025 to move to America in 2026/27.
SMART Objective #2
Specific : Search for possible cities for relocation, considering future job opportunities, average salary, cost of living and distance to relationships.

Measurable : Narrow down the search to three states using the same factors for each one to tally a pros and cons list.

Achievable : Allocate three hours weekly for research and seek advice from American connections. Narrow down locations options and have my girlfriend visit them for a better understanding of living conditions.

Relevant : Retrieving locations information and deciding ahead of time will allow the relocation process to be smooth and quick.

Time-bound : Finalise the list by June 2025, I will visit with my girlfriend over the next six months and make a decision by 2026.
Resources
Connect with Real Estate Agents: Contact multiple agents in advance to access new property listings for a smoother relocation.

Utilising Study Abroad Experience: Using my experience in the U.S. to quickly adapt to the new environment.
